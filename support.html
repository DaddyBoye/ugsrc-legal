2. About the App

The UGSRC App is the official mobile application of the Students’ Representative Council (SRC) of the University of Ghana. The App provides:

  • Official announcements and student notices
  • Access to PascoHub past examination questions
  • AI assistance through Ujamaa
  • Student-focused services and resources

3. Common Issues

  • Login or authentication errors
  • Access to PascoHub resources
  • AI assistant (Ujamaa) responses
  • App crashes or performance issues
  • Account deletion requests

4. Account & Data Requests
