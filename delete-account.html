Request Account Deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your UGSRC App account and associated personal data, in accordance with Google Play and Apple App Store policies.

How account deletion works

The UGSRC App does not support instant account deletion within the app. Instead, users can submit a deletion request via email.

  • Your request will be reviewed to verify account ownership.
  • Once approved, your account and personal data will be permanently deleted.
  • This action is irreversible.

Data that will be deleted

  • Account profile information (name, email, academic details)
  • Authentication records and session data
  • Feedback submissions and chat messages
  • Poll responses and event registrations
  • AI assistant (Ujamaa) conversation history
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Some anonymized or aggregated data may be retained where legally permitted.

How to submit a deletion request

Send an email from the email address associated with your UGSRC App account to:

gabrieladjeiboye@gmail.com

Please include the following details:

  • Full name
  • Email address used in the app
  • Program and level (optional but helpful)
  • A clear statement requesting account deletion
